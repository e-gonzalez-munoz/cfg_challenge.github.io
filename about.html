About Pet Adoption

Welcome to Pet Adoption Rocks, a place where our passion for animals and their welfare knows no bounds. Our journey began with the adoption of Roy, an ex-racing greyhound, from Wimbledon Greyhound Welfare in 2016. This heartwarming experience inspired us to do more for animals in need.

In 2019, our family welcomed Zebedee, a spirited Dalmatian, into our lives through Save a Spanish Spot. Zebedee's boundless energy and zest for life remind us every day of the joy that comes from rescuing and giving a loving home to animals.

Pet Adoption Rocks is committed to promoting compassion, building connections, and creating happy families through animal rescue. Join us in our mission to make the world a better place for all creatures.
